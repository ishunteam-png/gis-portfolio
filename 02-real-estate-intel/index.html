About

Each cell is 250 m × 250 m. The score combines foot traffic (shops + transit), residential density, tourist amenities, walkability (intersection density), and competition (penalty). The full model covers 8,415 cells over 504 km² — this dashboard shows the top 100 to keep the map readable.

Validated against the actual distribution of cafés in OSM: Spearman ρ = 0.39 — cells the model rates highly already have more cafés than cells it rates lowly.

Source: OpenStreetMap, OSMnx walk graph (62,849 intersection nodes for Tbilisi).
